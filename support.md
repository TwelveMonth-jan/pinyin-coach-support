# Support · 联系支持

## English

The fastest way to reach me is email: **[henrylaw2003@gmail.com](mailto:henrylaw2003@gmail.com)**

Please include:
- Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- The app version (Settings tab in the app, bottom)
- A short description of what went wrong, and what you expected

I usually reply within a few days.

### Common questions

**Q: My practice progress disappeared.**
The app stores everything on your device, so if you deleted and reinstalled the app, the data is gone — there's no cloud backup. iCloud sync may come in a future version.

**Q: How do I switch between Simplified and Traditional?**
On the Practice tab, tap the gear icon in the top right → Settings → toggle 简体 / 繁體.

**Q: I found a character with the wrong pinyin.**
Please email the character and what reading you expected. I'll fix it in the next update.

**Q: When will iPad / Mac support come?**
TBD. For now the app is iPhone-only because the IME-style candidate bar is designed around portrait phone layouts.

**Q: Why is there no audio?**
Audio is a planned future feature but requires licensing pronunciation files. V1 focuses on the typing pain point.

### 常见问题

**问：我的练习进度不见了。**
App 把所有数据存在设备上。如果你删除并重新安装，数据会一起消失——目前没有云端备份。iCloud 同步可能在未来版本加入。

**问：怎么切换简体和繁体？**
在练习页右上角点齿轮 → 设置 → 切换 简体 / 繁體。

**问：发现某个字的拼音不对。**
请邮件告诉我那个字和你期望的读音，下个版本会修正。

**问：什么时候支持 iPad / Mac？**
未定。目前只支持 iPhone，因为输入法候选栏的设计是围绕竖屏手机的。

**问：为什么没有发音？**
发音功能在计划中，但需要授权发音素材。V1 专注于打字这个痛点。
