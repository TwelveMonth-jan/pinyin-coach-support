# PinYin Coach
## 拼音练习

**Type Chinese the right way.** A pinyin spelling trainer for heritage speakers, dialect-region students, and anyone who hears Mandarin fluently but stumbles on the keyboard.

---

### What it does

- **Daily drills** — character mode (12/session) and phrase mode (8/session) with Leitner spaced repetition
- **Benchmark** — 10-phrase quick test to map your weak spots
- **Pinyin reference** — every valid syllable + a 1,500-character pinyin dictionary
- **Progress tracking** — by error category (zh/z, an/ang, n/l, ü→v, and 6 more)
- **Simplified and Traditional script** — toggle anytime
