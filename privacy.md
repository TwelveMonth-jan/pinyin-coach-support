# Privacy Policy — PinYin (拼音练习)

**Effective date:** May 13, 2026
**Last updated:** May 13, 2026
**Developer contact:** [henrylaw2003@gmail.com](mailto:henrylaw2003@gmail.com)

---

## English

### Summary

PinYin does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything you do in the app — practice attempts, scores, streaks, settings — stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

### Data we store on your device

The app uses Apple's on-device SwiftData framework to remember your progress so the spaced-repetition system works between sessions. Stored locally:

- Practice attempts (character, what you typed, whether it was correct, timestamp)
- Session records (date, total characters, correct count)
- Per-character Leitner box state and last-practiced date
- Per-phrase progress records
- Benchmark session results
- Preferences (script system, preferred practice mode, daily streak)

None of this leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

### Data we do not collect

- No name, email, phone number, or account
- No location, contacts, calendar, photos
- No advertising identifier (IDFA)
- No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
- No third-party tracking
- No network requests of any kind

### Children's privacy

PinYin is rated 4+ and is designed for primary and secondary school students. Because the app does not collect or transmit any data, it does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Parents can be confident their child's practice history remains private to the device.

### Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data — for example, adding optional cloud sync — we will update this page and bump the "Last updated" date. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.

### Contact

Questions about this policy: [henrylaw2003@gmail.com](mailto:henrylaw2003@gmail.com)

## 简体中文

### 摘要

「拼音练习」不收集、不传输、不出售任何个人数据。你在 app 内做的一切——练习记录、成绩、连续天数、设置——全部留在你的设备上。无账号、无服务器、无数据分析、无第三方 SDK。

### 我们在你设备上储存的内容

App 使用 Apple 的设备端 SwiftData 框架记录进度，让间隔复习系统跨会话工作。本地储存：

- 练习记录（字符、你输入的内容、是否正确、时间戳）
- 会话记录（日期、总字数、正确数）
- 每个字的 Leitner 复习盒状态与上次练习日期
- 每个短语的进度记录
- 基准测试结果
- 偏好设置（字体系统、练习模式、连续天数）

以上数据不会离开你的设备。卸载 app 即全部删除。

### 我们不收集的内容

- 不收集姓名、邮箱、电话号码、账号
- 不读取位置、通讯录、日历、照片
- 不使用广告标识符（IDFA）
- 不集成数据分析或崩溃报告 SDK
- 不进行任何第三方追踪
- 不发起任何网络请求

### 儿童隐私

「拼音练习」分级为 4+，面向中小学生设计。由于 app 不收集也不传输任何数据，我们不会主动收集 13 岁以下儿童（或你所在地区相应最低年龄）的信息。家长可放心，孩子的练习记录始终保留在设备上。

### 政策变更

如果未来 app 对数据的处理方式有变更（例如新增可选的云端同步），我们会更新本页并修改"最后更新"日期。重大变更也会在 app 的版本说明中告知。
